About

Hi, my name is Dotun. I'm a recent graduate of Nashville Software School. I may be new to software engineering, but I have years of experience and education in the world of information technology. After completing an undergraduate degree in Computer Science at the University of Lagos in Nigeria, I worked at different levels of IT across two continents, starting with training corporate executives on maximizing the capabilities of Microsoft Excel. And prior to my apprenticeship at Nashville Software School, I managed a centralized framework ensuring data integrity for a financial services firm.

Now armed with a full stack web-development training, coupled with a newly discovered love for creating beautiful UX experiences, I want to bridge the gap between functionality and design. When I'm not coding, I can be found reading books(currently slogging through the Game of Thrones series). I consider myself an amateur food conjurer, and an unrepentant foodie with a particular fondness for pancakes and hot cocoa.

villageSquare

Built using AngularJS and the Google Civic Data API, VillageSquare is a civic informational app that utilizes the users physical address to provide information on the elected public officials for that area. It also provides contact information for each official.

Check it out here: villageSquare

xhatter

xhatter is a fun chat app built using JavaScript and Firebase on the backend. On xhatter you get to pick the name of your favorite Superhero(or TV character) and then pretend to say something you think the character would say in a public chat room.
